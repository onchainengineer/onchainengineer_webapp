About
Introducing Pratyaksh, a highly talented and accomplished electrical and electronics engineering student with a deep passion for exploring the limitless possibilities of decentralized technology. Pratyaksh's impressive achievements include being recognized as a two-time MIT Covid-19 Challenge Winner by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, a Go Green 2021 Greater India Finalist by Schneider Electric, and a Clinton Global Initiative University Fellow by the Clinton Foundation.

With a strong understanding of the underlying principles of blockchain and a keen eye for innovation, Pratyaksh is always at the forefront of the latest developments in the field. From developing smart contract applications to delving into the intricacies of cryptography, he is committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible with blockchain technology and creating a more transparent and secure future for us all.

Pratyaksh is also deeply committed to using blockchain technology to create positive change in the world. His recent focus on FHE (Fully Homomorphic Encryption) based privacy-oriented open-source implementation of smart contracts, including their applications to decentralized health, energy, and identity, reflects this dedication. He is currently working on building VISCA: Verifiable Interoperable Secure Care Architecture, a project he started at the ETHIndia 2022 Hackathon and was recognized as the EY Starlight Technology Winner.
